I am passionate about communicating glacier and climate science to audiences of all ages and backgrounds and particularly engaging young people who are underrepresented in higher eduation. My outreach spans media engagement, curriculum development, and hands-on education from middle school to undergraduate level.

I am always happy to discuss potential outreach work — please find my contact details at the bottom of the page.

Some of my past outreach activities have involved: 

- **Ohio State Summer Data Camp** (2024 & 2025) — Developed and delivered data-analysis activities for middle and high school students
- **Westerville Central Earth Science Day** (2023, 2024, & 2025) — Annual visits to discuss research and career options with high school students
- **NBC4 Local Ohio** (2024) — Media interview on shrinking tropical glaciers following publication of Lamantia et al. (2024) in The Cryosphere
- **Ohio State University Press Release** (2024) — Featured in university news coverage linking El Niño to accelerated tropical ice loss
- **BBC Consultant** (2023) — Provided Andean glacier analysis and projection expertise for the upcoming documentary *The Americas*
- **McGraw Hill California Reveal Math® Scientist** (2024 - 2025) — Collaborated with McGraw Hill Publishing to create a 30-minute video on research and data analysis for middle school students
- **OSU Undergraduate Student Government Guest Speaker** (2024) — Presented on ice core and climate science research
- **OSU SELF Program** (2023) — Led ice core and climate science activity for first- and second-year minority undergraduate students
- **Byrd Polar Research Center Outreach Volunteer** (2021–2025) — Regular presentations, tours, and hands-on activities for groups ranging from elementary school to undergraduates
- **GHRO STEM Learning Day** (2022) — Organised an immersive STEM day for low-income middle and high school girls
- **Van Buren Middle School STEAM Day** (2022) — Virtual outreach presentation on glacier research and career paths
- **Bexley High School STEM Day** (2021) —  Presentations and panel with high school students about climate research and career options
